<h3 style="font-weight: 600;">HOW TO DISABLE SHARE OPTIONS OR PRICE ALERT NOTIFICATIONS IN MAGENTO?</h3>
<p>
For each specific Wishlist, your customers will be able to disable Share Options or Price Alert Notifications. These options will show up in the frontend under the <strong>Customer Account -> My Wish List</strong>, when editing a Wishlist.
</p>
<p>
<strong>Note:</strong> These options will only show up if the <strong>Share Wishlist</strong> and <strong>Price Alert</strong> functionalities are enabled in the Admin section under the following paths:
</p>
<p>
<strong>WeltPixel > Multiple Ajax Wishlist Settings > Enable Share Wishlist</strong>.
</p>
<ul>
	<p>
Note: If the Share Wishlist functionality is disabled, the Share buttons will be removed, and the people customers have already shared the wishlist with will no longer have access to it.
</p>
</ul>
<p>
WeltPixel > Multiple Ajax Wishlist Settings > Enable Price Alert.
</p>
<p>
How your customers can make use of this functionality:
</p>
<ul>
        <li>
            On the frontend, the user will need to log in to their account, and head into their Wishlists.
        </li>
        <li>
            On the Wishlist page, hover over the desired Wishlist and hit the <strong>Edit</strong> button.
            </li>
            <li>
                The user will be able to change the name of the Wishlist, as well as disable/enable the Share and Price Alert functionalities by setting the <strong>Disable Share</strong> and <strong>Disable Price Alert</strong> options to either <strong>No</strong> or <strong>Yes</strong>, respectively.
